About Us

Rivviet pronounced (Riv-ee-it) was started in April of 2026 by a group of individuals with the goal of creating software and development tools for fellow software devs and homelab users who care about open source transparency.


Rivviet is owned and operated by the subsidiary Learn To Homelab, a company founded in 2024 by Abe Peterson. Abe is an individual that started Learn To Homelab to bring free information technology to the masses with the goal of hands on FREE THOROUGH training content. His training focuses heavily on bringing security and privacy back to the individual and out of cloud environments.
